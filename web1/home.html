Charutar Vidya Mandal University (CVMU) is a prominent private university located in Vallabh Vidyanagar, Anand, Gujarat. Established in 2019 under the Gujarat Act No. 8 of 2009, CVMU is part of the Charutar Vidya Mandal (CVM) trust, which has been a pioneer in education since 1945. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 22 of the UGC Act, empowering it to confer degrees.
